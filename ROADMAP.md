### Technical Risks
- **Model accuracy:** Continuous training, validation
- **Scalability:** Cloud infrastructure, microservices
- **Data quality:** Validation pipelines, user feedback

### Business Risks
- **User adoption:** Farmer education, partnerships
- **Competition:** Unique social intelligence feature
