## 📊 **Branding Guidelines**

### **Name Usage**
- **Full Name:** GAINN (Global Agriculture Intelligence Neural Network)
- **Short Name:** GAINN
- **Acronym:** Always capitalize: GAINN (not Gainn or gainn)
- **In Code:** Use `gainn` (lowercase) for technical names

### **Examples**
✅ **Correct:**
- "Welcome to GAINN"
- "GAINN helps farmers make intelligent decisions"
- "The GAINN platform uses neural networks"
- Database: `gainn`
- Package: `gainn-frontend`

❌ **Incorrect:**
- "Welcome to Gainn"
- "gainn helps farmers" (in user-facing text)
- "The Gainn platform"

### **Taglines**
Primary: "Empowering farmers with intelligent decisions through neural networks"

Alternatives:
- "Global Agriculture Intelligence Neural Network"
- "Transforming farming from uncertainty into intelligent decision-making"
- "AI-powered agricultural intelligence for global farmers"
